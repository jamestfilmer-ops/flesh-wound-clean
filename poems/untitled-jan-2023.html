Poem

Untitled, Jan. 2023

Jan 21, 2023

A hardy jumble from the bones
Arises deep from the gut.
In glee, humorous thunder strikes
Beyond the threshold of norms.
The riffs and theories, the sharing
Of minds, and jolly embraces of our insanity
Give the mind ease, calming a misty world.
Temporarily, no doubt,
As the gloom and terror throws
Itself into our streets and living rooms.
But one good laugh suspends all respect
For the gloomy days ahead.
